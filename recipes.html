Sandwich

Sandwich with processed cheese

Ingredients
  • Slice of bread
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Sausage
  • Cheese
Steps
  1. Cut cucumber, tomato and sausage into round slices
  2. Put cucumber on bread
  3. Put tomato on tob of cucumber
  4. Put sausages on top of tomato
  5. Cut cheese
  6. Put cheese on top of sausage
  7. Put the sandwich in the microwave

Fried dumplings

Fried dumplings in creamy sauce

Ingredients
  • Dumplings
  • Two spoons of sour cream
  • One cup of water
  • A pinch of salt
  • Vegetable oil
Steps
  1. Pour vegetable oil into the frying pan
  2. Put dumplings in the frying pan
  3. Add salt
  4. Fry until dumplings are golden brown
  5. Mix sour crean with cup of water
  6. Add the resulting mass to the dumplings
  7. Wait for 5-7 minutes untill it is done
